Instrucciones

Controles

Utiliza las teclas de flecha o las teclas [W,A,S,D] para navegar con pacman.

Para pausar / reanudar el juego pulsa [ESPACIO] o [ESC] o simplemente haz clic en el área de juego.

Controles

Usa gestos de deslizamiento para navegar por pacman.

Alternativamente, utiliza los botones de flecha situados debajo del área de juego para navegar por pacman.

Para pausar / reanudar el juego, toca el área de juego una vez.

Hornos

Los hornos son criaturas que cazan a pacman y lo matarán si lo atrapan.

Cada horno tiene su propia estrategia para perseguir a pacman.

Inky

Inky will stay in the oven house until pacman has eaten at least 30 pills. His home is the bottom right corner.

Blinky

Blinky is the most agressive of the 4 ovens. He will start chasing pacman right away, and aim directly at him. His home is the upper right corner.

Pinky

Pinky will start chasing pacman right away, he will always aim 4 fields ahead and 4 fields left of pacman. His home is the upper left corner.

Clyde

Inky will stay in the oven house until pacman has eaten at least 2/3 of all pills. His home is the bottom left corner.

oven moods

The ovens have two different moods that change the way they act during the game.

Scatter mood

This is the default mood. When ovens are in scatter mood, they will just go to their home corner and stay there.

Chase mood

After a certain time the ovens change their mood and want to go chasing pacman. This is indicated through the walls turning red.

Items

Pills

The goal of every level is, to eat all the white pills without getting catched by the ovens. One pill results in 10 points.

Powerpills

In every level there are 4 powerpills, which are a bit bigger than the regular ones. If Pacman eats those, he will get strong enough to eat the ovens. You can see this indicated by the ovens turning blue. One powerpill results in 50 points.

Eating a oven results in 100 points. The soul of the oven will return to the oven house before starting to chase Pacman again.
